Groundwater Recharge

2021-Present

GIS based solution for groundwater recharge. The effective implementation of artificial recharge schemes depends on the identification of appropriate locations and structures.

Collaborating with: "Prof. Basant Maheshwari - Western Sydney University - Marvi (http://www.marvi.org.in/)" | "Student Mentors from ESRI India (https://www.esri.in/en-in/home)" | "Scientist from Central Ground Water Board (CGWB)"

Solution is also proposed by IIT Guwahati, India, and the Western Sydney University, Australia, for further research under the Australia-India Strategic Research Fund (AISRF) Round 14, funded by the Governments of Australia and India.

Here is the link to the Peer Review of the idea and the paper: https://tinyurl.com/2p8bfz2b

Carbon Footprint Label

2020-Present

I've created a proof of concept Blockchain technology-based solution to track carbon footprint and link them to Micro Carbon Credits as a financial instrument using Microsoft Azure and Ethereum ERC20 token system. The solution "Micro Carbon Credits-an incentive for sustainable consumption and production: A Conceptual Framework" has won the India National Level Microsoft Imagine Cup 2022 and Karnataka state-level innovation awards. I'm currently working on the solution at the National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal, under the mentorship of Prof. K V Gangadharan, Center for System Design department. This solution will help small and medium businesses trade these Micro Carbon Credit Tokens to incentivize the effort on low carbon footprint products and services, thus creating a new revenue stream.

Here is the link to the initial paper: https://tinyurl.com/2p8npb7f

Mentored by Dr. Mrityunjay Sahu, CFO and MD of a startup in the Carbon Credit Industry, Bariflo Labs (https://bariflo.teceads.co.in) and Prof. K V Gangadharan, Center for System Design, National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal.
